Green For Sudan: Pioneering Science Communication for a Sustainable Sudan

Building Hope Through Knowledge

Sudan stands at a crossroads — a nation rich in natural resources, human talent, and cultural resilience, yet deeply challenged by conflict, displacement, and environmental degradation.

Our Core Belief

Environmental advocacy is a necessity for peace, recovery, and justice.

Amid these struggles, the climate crisis intensifies droughts, floods, and food insecurity, threatening lives and livelihoods across the country. We recognize the urgency of this challenge.

At Green For Sudan (GFS), our mission is to amplify Sudanese voices through science communication — transforming complex environmental issues into stories and solutions that empower people and influence policy.

Our Focus

Empowering Every Sudanese Voice

At Green For Sudan, we believe that the key to overcoming our nation’s environmental and climate challenges lies in accessible scientific understanding.

Knowledge about our environment, natural resources, and sustainable practices is a fundamental right and a powerful tool for every citizen. Yet, this vital information often remains out of reach, limiting our ability to adapt, innovate, and build a resilient future.

Green For Sudan exists to change that. We strive to be the platform where complex scientific ideas are transformed into engaging, practical insights — created by and for Sudanese youth. Through our initiatives, we aim to nurture a generation of scientifically informed leaders who can design local solutions and champion sustainability.

You are not just the future — you are today’s innovators, problem-solvers, and changemakers. Empowering you with scientific literacy, connecting you to research, and amplifying your voices is the key to unlocking Sudan’s sustainable and peaceful future.

We believe in your potential — because Sudanese youth are unstoppable forces for good.

Who We Work With

Displaced & Refugees

We are particularly committed to hearing from those who have experienced displacement and refugee life — empowering them to take adaptive action in vulnerable conditions.

Students & Innovators

We train young Sudanese to be science communicators, equipping them to lead discussions, create awareness, and design evidence-based local solutions.

Farmers & Communities

We bridge science and society, helping communities understand environmental challenges and apply local, evidence-based solutions that improve livelihoods and resilience.

“From refugees to students, farmers, and urban youth — we see every Sudanese as a partner in building a sustainable future.”

Our Vision

We envision a future where young Sudanese voices are not only heard but are at the forefront of climate innovation and environmental resilience. A Sudan where a shared understanding of science bridges divides and empowers communities, guiding them toward a harmonious and sustainable relationship with their environment.

Our Strategic Goal

To achieve our vision and fulfil our mission, we are focused on:

Fostering a New Generation of Science Communicators

Why This Matters:

We believe that effective climate action begins with clear, compelling communication. By empowering young people to articulate environmental issues, we equip entire communities with the knowledge needed for informed decisions and collective action.

How We're Achieving It:

  • Expand Our Editorial Board: We are committed to training and mentoring a diverse team of young editors and writers to produce high-quality, scientifically accurate, and culturally relevant content.
  • Lead Engaging Campaigns: Through innovative digital initiatives, such as our "Climate Fact-Checker" campaign featuring our fictional character, Fatima, we make complex climate facts relatable, combat misinformation, and spark curiosity among our followers.
  • Conduct Capacity-Building Program: We have designed, hands-on programs focusing on climate science, advocacy, and building a strong cadre of youth leaders.

Stories to Tell

To concretely demonstrate this commitment and empower diverse voices, we present "Stories to Tell".

Through "Stories to Tell," we specifically mentor and amplify the voices of young Sudanese women, equipping them with the skills to document their unique resilience, innovation, and leadership in the face of environmental and humanitarian crises.

This digital initiative focuses on transforming complex climate realities and conflict experiences into compelling, human-centred narratives.

This special project directly showcases the power of science communication in advocating for impactful, gender-responsive solutions, turning lived experiences into a powerful testimony of resilience and a call for action.
